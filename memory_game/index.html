Instructions

Concentration — also knows as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso, or simply Pairs — is a card tame in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Why Play Concentration?

Concentration is a great way to exercise your brain and helps improve your memory. It's also fun!
